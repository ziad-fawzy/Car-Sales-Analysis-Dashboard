## Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of car sales data, focusing on market trends, customer preferences, and brand performance. The analysis aims to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights using Python's data science ecosystem.

## Key Features & Highlights
* **Logo-Shaped WordCloud:** Created a custom WordCloud using the company's logo as a mask, maintaining the original color scheme for a professional visual representation of brand dominance.
* **Unified Visual Styling:** Applied a consistent `Plasma` colormap across all charts and visualizations to ensure a cohesive and modern aesthetic throughout the notebook.
* **Time-Series Analysis:** Analyzed monthly sales trends from 2022 to 2024 to identify seasonality and growth patterns.
* **Gender-Based Preferences:** Explored how color choices and car models vary between different customer demographics.

## Technical Stack
* **Language:** Python 3.x
* **Libraries:** * `Pandas` (Data Cleaning & Manipulation)
    * `Matplotlib` & `Seaborn` (Advanced Data Visualization)
    * `WordCloud` (Text Data Visualization)
    * `NumPy` (Numerical Operations)

## Key Insights
* **Market Trends:** Identified a steady upward trend in sales with significant peaks during the fourth quarter of each year.
* **Brand Dominance:** The WordCloud and frequency analysis reveal that a few key brands hold the majority of the market share.
* **Price Distribution:** Found that the majority of sales fall within the mid-range price bracket, highlighting the most profitable customer segment.
